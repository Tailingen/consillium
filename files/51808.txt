Helicobacter Pylori and Proton Pump Inhibitor
The purpose of this study is to know the distribution of HP in stomach before use of PPI, during use of PPI, and after stop of PPI.
Helicobacter Infections
DRUG: PPI (proton pump inhibitor)
Analysis the change of Helicobacter pylori distribution according to the use of proton pump inhibitor, Change in the distribution of Helicobacter Pylori(HP) at 4 months.
Analysis the change of HP distribution according to the use of PPI in atrophic change of stomach, Change in the distribution of Helicobacter Pylori(HP) at 4 months.
Endoscopy and biopsy will be performed to investigate the distrubition of HP before, during, and after using PPI.
