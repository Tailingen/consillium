Proton Therapy for Chordomas and/or Chondrosarcomas
The purpose of this study is to collect information from medical records to see what effects proton beam radiation has on cancer and analyze possible side effects.
Chordomas|Chondrosarcomas
Collect and analyze outcome data on tumor control., When each patient has been followed for a minimum of 12 month and then again after 24 months to a maximum of 10 years.
Collect and analyze data on normal tissue morbidity., Specifically analyzing pituitary, visual and hearing function., When each patient has been followed for a minimum of 12 months and then again after 24 months to a maximum of 10 years.
Data collection will be obtained from the patient's medical records including initial evaluation, pathology report, dosimetry information, radiotherapy completion records and follow-up.
