The vessel resection and reconstruction of hepatic artery and portal vein in biliary tract cancer surgery is still controversial. In this study, we retrospectively evaluate the diagnostic image, pathology, peri-operative events, recurrence, and prognosis in these patients, as a multi-center surveillance.
