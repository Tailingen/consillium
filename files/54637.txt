Onco-geriatric Database of Patients With Pancreatic Cancer
This data base has been set in order to gather oncogeriatric information from elderly patients with pancreatic cancer who underwent an oncogeriatric evaluation.

ONCOGEPAN database gathers data from deux institutions (Institut Paoli-Calmettes and Assistance Publique des Hôpitaux de Marseille) involved in Marseille SIRIC (Integrated Cancer Research Site).
