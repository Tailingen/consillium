The purpose of this study is to describe the incidence, clinic characteristics, biological and suvirval in Lymphoblastic Leukemia patients in Mexico City reference hospitals.

Clinic characteristics, biological and survival in Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia patients in Mexico City reference hospitals, Date collection from clinical files with Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia diagnostic between 2009 and 2013 in Mexico City in 5 reference hospitals, 5 years
Multicenter and retrospective revision of clinical files with Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia diagnostic between 2009 and 2013 in Mexico City reference hospitals, in five hospitals with 50 and 100 new cases Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia per year.
