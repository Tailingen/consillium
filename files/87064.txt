Effect of TyG Index on Outcomes of Radical Prostatectomy
We investigate the association between Triglyserid-Glucose(TyG) index and prostate cancer in patients undergoing radical prostatectomy.
Metabolic Syndrome|Prostate Cancer|Insulin Resistance
Oncological outcomes, Effect of TyG index on oncological outcomes of Prostate cancer, 5 years
Survival, Death date of patient after radical prostatectomy, 5 years
TyG index is calculated with special formula. Patients undergoing radical prostatectomy are divided two groups according to the cut-off level of 8.55 for TyG index.Groups were compared statistically in terms of oncological outcomes and survival.
