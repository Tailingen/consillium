Urinary miRNA in Endometrial Cancer Study
This study is a prospective feasibility study with the addition of a retrospective cohort study on the the expression of microRNA in urine in endometrial cancer patients.
Endometrial Cancer
DIAGNOSTIC_TEST: Urinary miRNA
Accuracy of predictive value of miRNA test in detecting endometrial cancer, To determine if miRNA can distinguish endometrial cancer patients from healthy patients, 6 months
Correlation to final histology, To determine how well miRNA correlates with final histology, i.e. can miRNA determine the subtype of endometrial cancer, 6 months
