Phase 1b Study to Investigate the Safety and Maximum Tolerated Dose of Aldoxorubicin (INNO-206) Plus Doxorubicin HCL in Subjects With Advanced Solid Tumors
This is a phase 1b open-label study to investigate the safety and maximum tolerated dose of aldoxorubicin plus doxorubicin HCl adminstered as infusion every 3 weeks for up to 8 cycles in subjects with advance solid tumors.
Advanced Solid Tumor
DRUG: aldoxorubicin
Safety and Tolerability, The primary objective of this study is to determine the preliminary safety and maximum tolerated dose (MTD) of aldoxorubicin plus doxorubicin HCl in subjects with advanced solid tumors who have failed standard therapies., up to 6 months
Tumor Response, The secondary objective of this study is to evaluate the tumor response to aldoxorubicin plus doxorubicin HCl in this population assessed by radiographic means using the RECIST 1.1 criteria., up to 6 months
