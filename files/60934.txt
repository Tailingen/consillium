A Pathology Reference Laboratory for the Study of Infectious Diseases and Malignant Lymphoma
1. To establish a pathology reference laboratory for the consultation on the diagnosis of infectious diseases and lymphoma.
2. To study the specific pathogens on 100 cases of unexplained death due to infectious diseases in the files of Taiwan CDC and the Forensic Institutes of Department of Law and Justice.
3. To study the distribution of children lymphoid lesions in Vietnam and Taiwan and their virus association.
4. To establish a tissue bank for infectious diseases and lymphoma.
