Translation and validation of the BOMET-QoL-10 questionnaire in Germany and assessment of its validity and responsiveness.
Bone Metastasis
QoL, Validity and responsiveness of the BOMET-QoL-10 questionnaire in reference to EORTC QLQ-C30/BM-22, 6 months
Impact of characteristics on questionnaire scores, Impact of tumour entity, comorbidity and further parameters on the scores of bone metastasis at baseline, 6 months|Sensitivity of questionnaire, Sensitivity of BOMET-QoL-10 in patients with / without progression of the disease, 6 months|Sequence effects of questionnaires, Influence on the order questionnaires are filled out, 6 months
Aim of this study is to translate the questionnaire and to examine the psychometric properties of BOMET-QoL-10 in a German population. Correlations with the EORTC QLQ-C30/BM22 at the time of enrolment and throughout the study aim to verify that the BOMET-QoL-10 is reliable, valid and able to detect stabilization or changes in quality of life.
