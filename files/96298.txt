Collecting Patient-Reported Experiences With Radiation Dermatitis in Breast Cancer Patients Undergoing Radiotherapy
This study evaluates patient-reported experiences of radiation dermatitis in patients with breast cancer undergoing radiotherapy.
Breast Carcinoma
OTHER: Non-Interventional Study
Patient experiences with radiation dermatitis, Themes will be collected during a brief interview following agreement to participate. Common themes related to physical, emotional, and social impacts of radiation dermatitis will be categorized and quantified to provide a comprehensive understanding of the patient experience., Baseline (at enrollment)
