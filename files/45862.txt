Individual Patient Compassionate Use of GX-I7
Compassionate use of GX-I7 for patients with serious life-threatening illness that have exhausted all available therapies, with no other therapy options.
Glioblastoma|High Grade Glioma|Recurrent Glioblastoma|Melanoma|Advanced Cancer
DRUG: GX-I7
This program is being offered on a patient by patient basis and will require company, Institutional Review Board/Independent Ethics Committee and Single Patient IND approval.
